Nowadays, due to the constructions and expansion of cities, trees are exposed to destruction and destruction. Paying attention to the importance of the issue that trees play a very important role in preserving nature and human life, made the Palm campus group conduct its research to save the trees that are in the way of constructions, the boundaries of roads and highways, and the trees that were planted due to the change in the type of crops. Our Services

Supplying tall trees (between 50-400) that were destroyed during construction and replacing them in place - Supplying and planting large trees in parks, squares, streets, markets and residential and public areas and changing the face of urban green spaces In the shortest time - moving, transferring, growing and reviving old trees - saving part of the trees that are at risk of destruction due to construction and turning it into a new opportunity to continue life at the national level and in different climates
