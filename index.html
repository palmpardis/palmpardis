About

Nowadays, due to the constructions and expansion of cities, trees are exposed to destruction. Paying attention to the importance of the fact that trees play a very important role in preserving nature and human life, made Palm Pardis group conduct its research in order to save the trees that are in the way of constructions, the boundaries of roads and highways, and the trees that are planted due to the change in the type of crops.


We can move any type of tree of any shape and size from one place to another and guarantee the survival and health of that tree.

After more than ten years of research, experimental work and efforts, we succeeded in acquiring the necessary skills, knowledge, experience and technology to transfer and save more than +57,000 tree trunks, among them trees with a height of 25 meters and a diameter of more than 3 meters, some reaching the age of 350 years. Some of these trees are planted in different parts of the country and abroad with different climates and continue to live.


We are now holding a record in this regard.
